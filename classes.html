Liveliness

Many of us are involved in physical activities because we want to stay in shape and healthy. But, being physically fit and healthy is just one side of the regular workout coin. There are also other benefits. For instance, a flushed and fresh-looking skin. During exercises, our blood really gets flowing, which helps our bodies evenly distribute oxygen. While we exercise, our skin gets more of this powerful fuel, which helps it to regenerate, bloom, and stay younger.

Body Balance

Body Balance is an uplifting mix of yoga and pilates set to soothing music. If you're feeling stressed, it helps you become strong and stay calm. Body Balance is a series of moves designed to build strength, balance, and calm in a stressful world. This holistic workout takes you through some Yoga stretches and core-strengthening Pilates moves.

Air Yoga

Sure, you’ve heard or regular yoga, but what about trying out some new moves while floating feet into the air? Air yoga is an interdisciplinary workout that combines the flexibility and technique of regular yoga with the strength building of hanging. Whether you’ve tried air yoga before or are a total beginner, you’re welcome in our classes.
